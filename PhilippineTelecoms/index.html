Privacy Policy

Philippine Telecoms is a completely offline application -- no data ever leaves your phone. There are no automated analytics, crash reports, etcetera.

However, when you click on the sources of various prefixes, you will be taken to the official website or social media page of the telecom provider. In that event, I don't have control over their data collection policies anymore. You could visit their respective privacy policies, or ask them directly.
